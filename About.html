My name is Tim Hanson. I am a UX Designer based in Brooklyn, NY. Originally from Des Moines, Iowa, I earned my degree in business administration and marketing from Grand View University. I then went on to earn my certification in UX/UI Design from Denver University.

I have always considered myself a jack of all trades, master of none. I've pursued many professions in my life. I've worked as an assistant brewer and then a cook. I had a brief stint at the United States postal carrier. I've interned on a political campaign, and I've worked retail in the height of the COVID pandemic. It took me a long time find something that stuck. When I did that was UX Design. I am a creative and endlessly curious person and I wanted to work in something that fostered that. A friend of mine told me about UX design and it immediately piqued my interest. I was immediately interested in it. I entered a bootcamp and immersed myself in the field. Eight months later I started my first job as a UX Designer and I haven't looked back.

Having worked in varying and unpredictable environments I have grown adept at reading situations and stepping up into leadership roles or falling back and taking guidance. I have often worked closely with others seeing projects through beginning to end and have become very comfortable in team environments. With a business background, an eye for design, paired with a strong work ethic I can make myself a valuable asset to any team. I look forward to collaborating with you soon.
