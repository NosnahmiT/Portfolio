Consistency Updates for Mobile Navigation
UX/UI Professional Work

Over the past few years my company has been developing and rolling out their new design system. We have been designing and developing new systems with that design system and updating old systems with it as well. This project was a case of updating an older, outdated system with our design system. It was outdated aesthetically and functionally and the improvements made by updating it to our current design system standards were drastic. A year ago we had done preliminary research on our insured portal to see what users thought about the system and how we compared to competitors. That research provided a base for me to create low-fidelity wireframes, review them with stakeholders, test prototypes, and create a high-fidelity mockup ready for handoff to developers.
